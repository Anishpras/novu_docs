[Maqsam](https://maqsam.com/) is the friendliest cloud telephony solution for SMEs.

# Getting Started

To use the Maqsam provider in the email channel, you will need to create a Maqsam account and add your access key & secret to the Maqsam integration on the Novu platform.
Contact `support@maqsam.com` to enable the API feature for your account, then you can generate `access_key_id` and `access_secret` combination from the account settings.

# Creating the Maqsam integration with Novu

- Visit the [Integrations](https://web.novu.co/integrations) page on Novu.
- Select Maqsam service
- Click `Next`
- Choose your preferred deployment environment `Development`\`Production`. Then click `CREATE`.
- Enter your Maqsam `access_key_id` & `access_secret`.
- Click on the `Disabled` button and mark it as `Active`.
- Click on the **Update** button.
- You should now be able to send notifications using Maqsam in Novu.
